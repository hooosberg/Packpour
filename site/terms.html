    Use Packpour as a responsible operator.

    Packpour is a tool that assists with metadata filling. These terms define the boundaries of intended use and the scope of what the software does.

    What Packpour does

    Packpour is a Chrome browser extension that reads locally imported TXT or Markdown metadata packs and fills visible editable fields in App Store Connect after a direct user action. It does not submit, publish, or approve any content automatically.

    Intended use

    Packpour is intended for app developers, ASO professionals, and localization teams who work with App Store Connect metadata. By using this extension you agree that you are authorized to access and edit the App Store Connect accounts and apps you operate the extension on.

    What Packpour does not do

    • It does not click Save, Publish, or Submit for Review on your behalf.
    • It does not access, read, or transmit App Store Connect credentials.
    • It does not fill password, payment, token, or secret-looking fields.
    • It does not operate outside appstoreconnect.apple.com.
    • It does not send any data to external servers.
